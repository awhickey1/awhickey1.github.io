About me

Hi, I am always challenging how things can be better and exploring topics that I am curious about. I have created a successful non-profit, invented a nationally awarded rotary combustion engine, and founded a funded startup. I have worked in highly technical positions such as in semiconductor manufacturing and advanced engine research at Intel Corporation and Ford Motor Company, respectively. Additionally, I am have worked on self-driven projects incorporating websites, apps, and microprocessors -- C, C++, R, and MATLAB are my strongest programming languages.

My industry interests are mainly in B2B and B2C software or hardware. I previously graduated from Stanford University (MS in mechanical engineering, depth in Mechatronics) and from Arizona State University (BSE in mechanical engineering and the esteemed Outstanding 2016 Engineering Graduate).
