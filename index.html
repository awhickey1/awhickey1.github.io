Andrew Hickey is an innovator and leader who is motivated to make a positive impact in society. He is currently pursuing a M.S. in mechanical engineering at Stanford University. He graduated from Arizona State University with a B.S.E. in mechanical engineering, a 4.0 GPA and as the esteemed outstanding graduate from Ira A. Fulton Engineering School in May 2016.

His favorite components from his experiences are leading groups of people (starting non-profit, TinkTank & Founder of organizations), thinking of innovative solutions (National Silver Medalist in Engineering Technology, $100k grant to create a product for people who are visually impaired, & won seed money for a start up--BeeSprout), and connecting with a diverse range of people (Success Coach & Zip Line Guide). His industry experience with Intel, Tallwave Capital, and Ford Motor Company challenged him to independently lead projects and deliver impactful results for clients, research and products. He has a track record that illustrates his capabilities along with diverse, highly regarded awards and being recognized as one of the top 30 Lambda Chi Alpha members under the age of 30.

In his free time, he enjoys playing sports, traveling, mentoring and learning something new.
